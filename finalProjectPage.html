Ella's Final Project!

For my final Project I created a user friendly app that allows people to explore drawing in a simple way. In my program,
I included an erase button because I wanted people to have the option to alter their picture, or they can utilize this
aspect if they make a mistake. The user has many color options they can use to create their picture. For some people,
creative activities are challenging. Therefore, this app includes an option where you can they can be given a suggestion
of what to draw! Likewise, for young children this app can be an interative actvity because they can chose to receive positive
feedback on their drawings. The app has the duel benefit of creative time use as well as possibly triggering an interest in computer
science. Although this program is simple, it is an easy way to be creative. Creativity does not have to be complicated, it can be simple.
Likewise, in my program you cannot draw outside the white rectangle, as this is more similar to how a realistic program would be modeled.
