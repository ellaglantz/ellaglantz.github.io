Ella's Final Project!

For my final Project I created a user freindly app that allows people to explore drawing in a simple way.
This app allows mistakes to happen, because an erase button is included! The user has many color options they can use to create their picture.
For some people, creative activities are challenging. Therefore, this app includes an option where you can be given a suggestion of what to draw!
Likewise, for young children this app can be interatcive because they can recieve positive feedback on their drawings with just on click. Children spend
alot of time on devices, so this app allows them to spend their time doing something craetve while also
feeling confident in their artistic abilities because they receive feedback!
This is a great way for children to spend free time doing something productive. Likewise, a child learning to be confindnet
is necessary for future development, and this app can help with that!
