Ella's Final Project!

For my final Project I created a user friendly app that allows people to explore drawing in a simple way.
In my app I included an erase button because I wanted people to have the option to alter their picture,
or they can utilize this aspect if they make a mistake. The user has many color options they can use to create their
picture. For some people, creative activities are challenging. Therefore, this app includes an option where you can
be given a suggestion of what to draw! Likewise, for young children this app can be an interatcive actvity considering
they can recieve positive feedback on their drawings with just one click. The app has the duel benefit of creative time use
and growing your child's confindence. This is a great way for children to spend free time doing something productive.
Likewise, a child's confidence growing and developing is necessary for future development, and this app can help with that!
